A Vision of Excellence

From a simple idea to the premier indoor golf destination in the region.

Founded in 2020

What began as a passion project among golf enthusiasts has evolved into the region's most sophisticated indoor golf experience. Our founders envisioned a space where technology meets tradition, creating the perfect environment for golfers of all levels.

Today, we proudly host over 10,000 players annually, from beginners taking their first swing to professionals perfecting their game.
