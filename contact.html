QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our facility and services.

Do I need to bring my own clubs?

No, we provide premium club sets for all skill levels at no extra charge. You're also more than welcome to bring your own clubs if you prefer to practice with your personal gear.

What is the recommended attire?

Casual golf attire or comfortable athletic wear is preferred. To protect our simulation turf, we ask that you do not wear metal spikes; clean sneakers or soft-spiked golf shoes are perfect.

Is food and drinks available in the bays?

Yes! Our full restaurant and bar menu is available. You can order gourmet meals, snacks, and specialized cocktails directly to your private bay using our digital ordering system.

Can beginners play at the lounge?

Absolutely! Our simulators are pre-loaded with "Fun Modes" and games perfect for newcomers. We also offer beginner coaching programs to help you build confidence and learn basic mechanics.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 days in advance for peak times like weekend evenings. Members enjoy priority access and can book up to 30 days in advance.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer digital and physical gift cards in any denomination. They can be redeemed for bay rentals, professional lessons, or merchandise in our pro shop.
