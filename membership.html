Plan Details & FAQ

How do I book?

Members get a dedicated dashboard for quick 1-click bookings. Simply log in, select your preferred bay and time, and your booking is confirmed instantly.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, and the price difference is prorated for your current billing cycle.

Is there a long-term commitment?

Monthly plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled at any time before your next billing date. Quarterly and Yearly plans are prepaid for the term and offer significant discounts compared to monthly billing.

Are Pro Lessons included in all plans?

Pro and Elite plans includes one 30-minute pro session monthly. Basic members can book pro sessions separately at a discounted member rate.
